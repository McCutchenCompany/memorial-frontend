How it Works

Share Memories

Memorial provides a place for you to share memories of who your loved one was. Every memory added paints a deeper picture of the life you want to remember. Sharing the memorial on social media is a great way to let others know they can began adding their too. As the memorial owner you decide if you want to approve the memories before they are displayed, or if anyone can post their memories without having to wait for your approval.

Location

You decide where the memorial should be located. It can be placed digitally anywhere in the world for people to view from their phones, tablets, or computers. Anyone near your memorial will see it displayed on their search page under "Memorials near you". Imagine placing it at their final resting place and instead of visitors seeing a name and a couple of dates, they are able to see a glimps into the life of your loved one.

Timeline

Create an interactive timeline celebrating your loved one's life. Each life event you add helps tell their story as photos and video bring it to life. This is where their personality really shines through. What better way for future generations to connect with those that came before.
