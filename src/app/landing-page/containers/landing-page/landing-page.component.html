How it Works

Share Memories

Memorial provides a place for anyone to share memories of who your loved one was. Every memory added paints a deeper picture of the life you want to remember. Sharing the memorial on social media is a great way to let others know they can begin adding theirs too. As the memorial owner you decide if you want to approve the memories before they are displayed, or if anyone can post their memories without having to wait for your approval.

Location

You decide where the memorial should be located. It can be placed digitally anywhere in the world for people to view from their phones, tablets, or computers. Anyone near your memorial will see it displayed on their search page under "Memorials near you". Imagine placing it at their final resting place and instead of visitors seeing a name and a couple of dates, they are able to see a glimpse into the life of your loved one.

Timeline

Create an interactive timeline celebrating your loved one's life. Each life event you add helps tell their story as photos and videos bring it to life. Visitors will be able to click through each event and be transported back in time as they read about his/her experiences. What better way for future generations to connect with those that came before.

Shared Photo Album

Each Memorial has a shared photo album where anyone can upload their photos. Now there is one place where all of the photos of your loved one can live. Here they will be safe from fire, floods, theft, or any number of other life events that can ruin your physical copy. Since anyone can upload their photos, you'll probably see some that you had no idea existed! Each Memorial owner can decide if they need to review the photos before they are published.
